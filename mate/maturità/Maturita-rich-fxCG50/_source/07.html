07 - Derivate e tangenti

Definizione

\[f'(x_0)=\lim_{h\to0}\frac{f(x_0+h)-f(x_0)}h\]

Tangente in (x_0):

\[y=f(x_0)+f'(x_0)(x-x_0)\]

Normale:

\[m_n=-\frac1{f'(x_0)}\]

Regole

\[(f\pm g)'=f'\pm g'\]

\[(fg)'=f'g+fg'\]

\[\left(\frac fg\right)'=\frac{f'g-fg'}{g^2}\]

\[(f(g(x)))'=f'(g(x))g'(x)\]

Derivate base

\[D(x^\alpha)=\alpha x^{\alpha-1}\]

\[D(e^x)=e^x,\quad D(a^x)=a^x\ln a\]

\[D(\ln x)=\frac1x,\quad D(\log_a x)=\frac1{x\ln a}\]

\[D(\sin x)=\cos x,\quad D(\cos x)=-\sin x\]

\[D(\tan x)=\frac1{\cos^2x}=1+\tan^2x\]

Inverse trig

\[D(\arcsin x)=\frac1{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\]

\[D(\arccos x)=-\frac1{\sqrt{1-x^2}}\]

\[D(\arctan x)=\frac1{1+x^2}\]

Teoremi

Rolle: (f(a)=f(b)c:f’(c)=0).

Lagrange:

\[\exists c:\ f'(c)=\frac{f(b)-f(a)}{b-a}\]

De L’Hospital:

\[\lim\frac fg=\lim\frac{f'}{g'}\]
