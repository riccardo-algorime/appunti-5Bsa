# 06 - Limiti e continuita

## Algebra dei limiti
Se \(f\to l\), \(g\to m\):

$$f\pm g\to l\pm m,\quad fg\to lm,\quad \frac fg\to\frac lm\ (m\ne0)$$

## Forme indeterminate
$$\frac00,\quad \frac\infty\infty,\quad 0\cdot\infty,\quad \infty-\infty,\quad 1^\infty,\quad 0^0,\quad \infty^0$$

## Limiti notevoli
$$\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\sin x}{x}=1$$

$$\lim_{x\to0}\frac{1-\cos x}{x^2}=\frac12$$

$$\lim_{x\to0}\frac{e^x-1}{x}=1$$

$$\lim_{x\to0}\frac{\ln(1+x)}{x}=1$$

$$\lim_{x\to0}\frac{a^x-1}{x}=\ln a$$

$$\lim_{x\to\infty}\left(1+\frac1x\right)^x=e$$

## Infinitesimi equivalenti
Per \(x\to0\):

$$\sin x\sim x,\quad \tan x\sim x,\quad 1-\cos x\sim\frac{x^2}{2}$$

$$e^x-1\sim x,\quad \ln(1+x)\sim x$$

## Asintoti
Verticale:

$$\lim_{x\to x_0}f(x)=\pm\infty \Rightarrow x=x_0$$

Orizzontale:

$$\lim_{x\to\pm\infty}f(x)=l \Rightarrow y=l$$

Obliquo:

$$m=\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{f(x)}x,\qquad q=\lim_{x\to\infty}(f(x)-mx)$$

$$y=mx+q$$
