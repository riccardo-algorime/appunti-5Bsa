# 06 - Esempio: derivate

## Derivata prima
$$f(x)=x+4x^{-2}$$

$$f'(x)=1-8x^{-3}=1-\frac8{x^3}$$

$$f'(x)=\frac{x^3-8}{x^3}$$

Punto critico:

$$f'(x)=0\Rightarrow x^3-8=0\Rightarrow x=2$$

## Segno di \(f'\)
Intervalli:

$$(-\infty,0),\quad(0,2),\quad(2,+\infty)$$

Segni:

$$f'>0\text{ in }(-\infty,0)$$

$$f'<0\text{ in }(0,2)$$

$$f'>0\text{ in }(2,+\infty)$$

Quindi:

$$f\nearrow(-\infty,0),\quad f\searrow(0,2),\quad f\nearrow(2,+\infty)$$

Minimo:

$$x=2,\qquad f(2)=2+\frac44=3$$

$$\boxed{(2,3)}$$
