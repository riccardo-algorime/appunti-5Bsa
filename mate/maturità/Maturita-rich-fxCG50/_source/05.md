# 05 - Coniche

## Circonferenza
Centro \(C(\alpha,\beta)\), raggio \(r\):

$$ (x-\alpha)^2+(y-\beta)^2=r^2 $$

Forma generale:

$$x^2+y^2+ax+by+c=0$$

$$C\left(-\frac a2,-\frac b2\right)$$

$$r=\sqrt{\frac{a^2+b^2}{4}-c}$$

Tangente in \(T(x_T,y_T)\):

$$ (x_T-\alpha)(x-x_T)+(y_T-\beta)(y-y_T)=0 $$

## Parabola
Asse verticale:

$$y=ax^2+bx+c$$

Vertice:

$$V\left(-\frac b{2a},-\frac\Delta{4a}\right)$$

Asse:

$$x=-\frac b{2a}$$

## Ellisse
$$\frac{x^2}{a^2}+\frac{y^2}{b^2}=1$$

Se \(a>b\):

$$c=\sqrt{a^2-b^2},\quad F(\pm c,0),\quad e=\frac ca$$

## Iperbole
$$\frac{x^2}{a^2}-\frac{y^2}{b^2}=1$$

Asintoti:

$$y=\pm\frac ba x$$

Fuochi:

$$c=\sqrt{a^2+b^2},\quad F(\pm c,0)$$

## Omografica
$$y=\frac{ax+b}{cx+d}$$

Asintoti:

$$x=-\frac dc,\qquad y=\frac ac$$
